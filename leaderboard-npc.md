# Capacity Leaderboard NPC

The Capacity Leaderboard NPC stands beside the **Top Capacity** leaderboard in the lobby. Right-click the NPC named **✦ Claim Capacity rewards here!** to receive a reward based on your current Capacity leaderboard position.

The NPC uses a fixed skin. Its appearance and name do not change when a different player reaches first place.

## Rewards

Your position is checked again whenever you right-click the NPC.

| Current position | Reward |
| --- | --- |
| #1–#3 | 5 Enchanted Golden Apples |
| #4–#10 | 5 Golden Apples |
| #11–#20 | 10 Golden Carrots |
| #21 or lower | 20 Apples |

Operators are not included in the reward ranking. Players who are not yet eligible receive a message instead of a reward.

## Claim Cooldown

Each player can claim once every **eight hours**. The cooldown is personal and starts after the complete reward has been added to your inventory.

Right-clicking during the cooldown shows the remaining hours and minutes. Your next reward is based on your position at the time of the next claim, not the position from your previous claim.

## Inventory Space

Leave enough inventory space for the complete reward before claiming. If it does not fit, the claim is rejected and the eight-hour cooldown does not start.
