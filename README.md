## Quick Start

1. Equip a full set of iron armor at least once.
2. Complete the Verdant dungeon to unlock your Master Chest.
3. Open it with `/mc`.
4. Shift-click items from your inventory into the Master Chest menu to store them.
5. Left-click a stored item to take up to one full stack, or right-click it to take one item.

Use `/roadmap` whenever you want to see your next progression goal.

After reaching Atom rank, use `/cw` to open the Capacity World menu.
