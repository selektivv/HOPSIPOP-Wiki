# Capacity

Capacity is both your Master Chest item limit and the resource used for [ranks](ranks.md), claims, and mining upgrades. One Capacity stores one individual item, so a stack of 64 uses 64 Capacity.

## Checking Capacity

- `/playtime` shows the current item limit.
- Master Chest Shortcuts shows stored items and free space.
- `/perks` shows [rank](ranks.md) progress.

## Earning Capacity

Capacity comes from progression activities, voting rewards, events, eligible mob kills, and repeatable mining [counter](capacity-world/counters.md) goals.

## Spending Capacity

[Claims](claims.md) and [mining upgrades](capacity-world/upgrades.md) deduct from the same total. Upgrade purchases are permanent; released claim area is refunded.

Spending never deletes stored items. If the new limit is below the stored amount, new deposits stop until items are removed or more Capacity is earned.

Before spending, check free storage and the next [rank](ranks.md) requirement.
