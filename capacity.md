# Capacity

Capacity is the shared resource that connects storage, ranks, claims, mining, and upgrades. It is not limited to the Capacity World: every change can affect several parts of your progression at once.

Start with the [system overview](system-overview.md) if you want to see the complete relationship between these features.

## Storage Limit

One Capacity represents room for one individual item in your Master Chest network. A stack of 64 blocks therefore uses 64 Capacity. Different item types do not receive separate limits.

Capacity belongs to the virtual network rather than to a placed chest. Every access point for the same network uses the same stored-item total and the same limit.

If your stored item count reaches the limit, further deposits fail until you remove items or earn more Capacity.

## Checking Your Capacity

- Run `/playtime` to see your current item limit.
- Open the [storage network](master-chest/README.md) and hover over Shortcuts to compare stored items with free space.
- Open `/perks` to see your current [rank and rank progress](master-chest/capacity-and-progression.md#capacity-ranks).
- Open `/roadmap` to review progression activities and feature unlocks.

## Earning Capacity

Capacity can be earned through HOPSIPOP progression and activities. Current sources include dungeon milestones, eligible Masterlands mob kills, [mining-counter goals](capacity-world/mining-and-rewards.md), voting rewards, daily-event prizes, Vote Crate rewards, and other server rewards.

The mining world provides a repeatable source. [Counter Boosts](capacity-world/boosts-and-notifications.md) can temporarily increase the personal reward from completed goals.

## Spending Capacity

Some actions deduct Capacity as currency:

- [Mining upgrades](capacity-world/upgrades.md), including Counter Reduction, Lucky Break, Co-op Size, Chunk Drill, and Fluid Collector unlocks.
- [Creating or expanding land claims](claims.md) outside the temporary mining world.

Deleting or shrinking claim area refunds the released claim cost. Capacity spent on permanent mining upgrades is not refunded.

## How Spending Affects Storage and Ranks

Spending reduces your current Capacity immediately. This can lower your storage limit, reduce rank progress, or remove a rank if you fall below its requirement.

Stored items are not deleted when the new limit becomes lower than the number already stored. However, deposits can stop until the network is brought below the reduced limit or more Capacity is earned.

Before a large purchase or claim expansion:

1. Check the stored-item total and free space in Master Chest Shortcuts.
2. Check the current and next rank requirements in `/perks`.
3. Leave enough room for [automation output](master-chest/automation.md) and [returning Chunk Drills](capacity-world/chunk-drills.md).
