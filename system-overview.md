# How the HOPSIPOP System Fits Together

HOPSIPOP's storage and mining features are parts of one progression loop. Items flow into your storage network, [Capacity](capacity.md) determines the network limit and rank progress, mining earns more [Capacity](capacity.md), and advanced tools send their results back into storage.

## The Connected Loop

**Progression unlocks storage → activities earn [Capacity](capacity.md) → [Capacity](capacity.md) expands storage and ranks → Atom unlocks the mining world → mining earns more [Capacity](capacity.md) → upgrades and automation feed items back into storage.**

## System Map

| System | Main role | Connected systems |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Master Chest storage network | Stores and organizes items virtually. | Its item limit is your [Capacity](capacity.md); OmniSync, automation, hoppers, and Chunk Drills use the network. |
| [Capacity](capacity.md) | Sets storage space, rank progress, and spending power. | Earned through progression and mining; spent on claims and mining upgrades. |
| [Capacity](capacity.md) ranks | Unlock perks and later features. | Atom opens the mining world; higher ranks improve or unlock connected tools. |
| [Capacity](capacity.md) World | Provides repeatable mining goals and [Capacity](capacity.md) rewards. | Uses ranks, upgrades, boosts, co-op groups, and Chunk Drills. |
| [Claims](claims.md) | Protect land outside the temporary mining world. | Claim area spends [Capacity](capacity.md); released area refunds it. |
| OmniSync and automation | Move or process items with less manual work. | Pull from and return results to the selected Master Chest network. |
| Chunk Drills | Excavate temporary-world chunks automatically. | Advance the mining counter and send produced drops to Master Chest storage. |

## Storage Comes First

Complete the early progression steps and the Verdant dungeon to unlock the [Master Chest storage network](master-chest/getting-started.md). The network is virtual: `/mc`, portable Master Chests, and placed access points can all lead to the same stored contents.

The number of individual items the network can hold is controlled by [Capacity](capacity.md). This is not a separate mining-only score; it is the shared resource connecting storage, ranks, claims, and upgrades.

## Capacity Drives Progression

Earning [Capacity](capacity.md) increases storage space and moves you toward the next [Capacity rank](master-chest/capacity-and-progression.md#capacity-ranks). Ranks unlock benefits across the connected system.

[Capacity](capacity.md) can also be spent. [Claim expansion](claims.md) and [mining upgrades](capacity-world/upgrades.md) reduce the same value used for storage and ranks, so a purchase can lower free storage space or even remove a rank.

## Mining Feeds Storage

At Atom rank, `/cw` opens the [mining-world menu](capacity-world/getting-started.md). Completing mining-counter goals awards [Capacity](capacity.md). Boosts change personal rewards, co-op groups share progress, and upgrades make goals faster or unlock automation.

[Chunk Drills](capacity-world/chunk-drills.md) complete the loop: they mine temporary-world chunks, advance the [Capacity](capacity.md) counter, and transfer produced drops into the owner's Master Chest network.

## Permanent and Temporary Parts

| Permanent player progress | Temporary mining-world state |
| --- | --- |
| Earned [Capacity](capacity.md) | Terrain and player-built structures |
| [Capacity](capacity.md) ranks | Current block-counter progress at reset |
| Purchased mining upgrades | Active co-op groups at reset |
| Master Chest contents | Placed containers and uncollected world items |
| Personal settings and boost expiry | Active world layout and mined chunks |

The [Capacity](capacity.md) World resets every ten hours. Read [World Rules and Reset Safety](capacity-world/world-rules-and-resets.md) before building, storing items, or leaving Chunk Drills active near a reset.
