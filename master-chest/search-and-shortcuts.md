# Search, Categories, and Shortcuts

## Item Categories

The category tabs narrow the current view without moving or changing any stored items.

- All
- Building
- Colored
- Natural
- Functional
- Redstone
- Tools
- Combat
- Food
- Ingredients

Select a category to view only matching items. Use Back to return to the complete overview. Items follow Minecraft's creative-menu style ordering, making large networks easier to scan.

## Chat Search

Chat Search is optional and starts disabled.

1. Open `/shortcuts` or click Shortcuts in the Master Chest menu.
2. Click Chat Search to turn it on.
3. Mention an item you are looking for in chat.
4. If a matching stored item is found, answer `yes` to open a filtered Master Chest view or `no` to dismiss the suggestion.

Chat Search checks your own network and networks shared with you. Because it reacts to item words in ordinary chat, leave it disabled if you prefer not to receive suggestions.

## Shortcuts Menu

The Shortcuts button also shows the number of stored item types, the total item count, the [capacity](../capacity.md) limit, and remaining free space. Its menu links to other HOPSIPOP features, including:

- Claims
- Cell Tower
- Perks and rank progress
- Guides
- Shared Chests
- MasterPets
- Fishing
- Scoreboard visibility
- Auctions
- [Capacity](../capacity.md) World

Some shortcuts remain locked until their progression or rank requirement is met.

## Portable Workbench and Ender Chest

The main Master Chest menu gains extra buttons as you progress:

- Reach wave 21 in the Infinity Arena to unlock the portable Workbench.
- Reach wave 50 in the Infinity Arena to unlock Ender Chest access.

These buttons open the relevant interface directly from the Master Chest menu.
