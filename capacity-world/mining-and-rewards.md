# Mining, Counters, and Rewards

## The Basic Mining Loop

By default, every ten eligible blocks you mine complete one block goal and award one [Capacity](../capacity.md). The action bar above your hotbar shows your current counter progress.

When a goal is completed, the counter returns to zero and the reward is added immediately. Progress beyond the goal does not carry into the next goal.

## Blocks That Count

Most blocks that can be mined normally in the [Capacity](../capacity.md) World count. A break must succeed; cancelled block breaks do not advance the counter.

Cobblestone and Basalt are specifically excluded. Mining either one gives no counter progress.

## [Capacity](../capacity.md) Rewards

- A normal completed goal awards one [Capacity](../capacity.md).
- An active [Counter Boost](boosts-and-notifications.md) awards three [Capacity](../capacity.md) to the boosted player instead.
- [Co-op partners](coop.md) normally receive one [Capacity](../capacity.md) when the shared goal is completed.
- [Capacity](../capacity.md) earned here immediately changes your Master Chest limit and [Capacity](../capacity.md)-rank progress.

The Enter [Capacity](../capacity.md) World button records and displays the total [Capacity](../capacity.md) you have earned through this world.

## Counter Reduction

[Counter Reduction](upgrades.md#counter-reduction) permanently lowers your personal block goal. It begins at ten eligible blocks and can eventually be reduced to one block per goal.

In a co-op, the shared counter uses the current group owner's goal. See [Co-op Mining](coop.md) before planning upgrades with a group.

## Lucky Break

[Lucky Break](upgrades.md#lucky-break) applies to blocks you mine manually. When it triggers, that block contributes a random one to five points of counter progress instead of the usual one.

A trigger can still roll only one point. Any amount that passes the current goal is discarded when the counter resets, so a large roll near the end of a goal may not provide its full possible value.

Lucky Break does not apply to blocks mined by Chunk Drills.

## Chunk Drill Progress

Every eligible solid block removed by one of your [Chunk Drills](chunk-drills.md) adds exactly one point to the relevant counter. Counter Reduction, [co-op rewards](coop.md), and an active [Counter Boost](boosts-and-notifications.md) still affect completed goals, but Lucky Break does not multiply drill progress.

## Checking Progress

- Watch the action bar for the current counter and active boost time.
- Watch the [Capacity](../capacity.md) World scoreboard for the next reset.
- Use `/perks` to see how earned [Capacity](../capacity.md) affects your rank.
- Use `/playtime` or Master Chest Shortcuts to check your current storage limit and available space.
