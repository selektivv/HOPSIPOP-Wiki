# Mining, Counters, and Rewards

## The Basic Mining Loop

By default, every ten eligible blocks you mine complete one block goal and award one Capacity. The action bar above your hotbar shows your current counter progress.

When a goal is completed, the counter returns to zero and the reward is added immediately. Progress beyond the goal does not carry into the next goal.

## Blocks That Count

Most blocks that can be mined normally in the Capacity World count. A break must succeed; cancelled block breaks do not advance the counter.

Cobblestone and Basalt are specifically excluded. Mining either one gives no counter progress.

## Capacity Rewards

- A normal completed goal awards one Capacity.
- An active Counter Boost awards three Capacity to the boosted player instead.
- Co-op partners normally receive one Capacity when the shared goal is completed.
- Capacity earned here immediately changes your Master Chest limit and Capacity-rank progress.

The Enter Capacity World button records and displays the total Capacity you have earned through this world.

## Counter Reduction

Counter Reduction permanently lowers your personal block goal. It begins at ten eligible blocks and can eventually be reduced to one block per goal.

In a co-op, the shared counter uses the current group owner's goal. See [Co-op Mining](coop.md) before planning upgrades with a group.

## Lucky Break

Lucky Break applies to blocks you mine manually. When it triggers, that block contributes a random one to five points of counter progress instead of the usual one.

A trigger can still roll only one point. Any amount that passes the current goal is discarded when the counter resets, so a large roll near the end of a goal may not provide its full possible value.

Lucky Break does not apply to blocks mined by Chunk Drills.

## Chunk Drill Progress

Every eligible solid block removed by one of your Chunk Drills adds exactly one point to the relevant counter. Counter Reduction, co-op rewards, and an active Counter Boost still affect completed goals, but Lucky Break does not multiply drill progress.

## Checking Progress

- Watch the action bar for the current counter and active boost time.
- Watch the Capacity World scoreboard for the next reset.
- Use `/perks` to see how earned Capacity affects your rank.
- Use `/playtime` or Master Chest Shortcuts to check your current storage limit and available space.
