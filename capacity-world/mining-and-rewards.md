# Mining Rewards

Completing a mining [counter](counters.md) goal normally awards one [Capacity](../capacity.md). The reward is added immediately and increases storage space and [rank](../ranks.md) progress.

## Reward Modifiers

- An active [Counter Boost](counter-boosts.md) changes the boosted player's reward to three.
- In a [co-op](coop.md), every member receives one when the shared goal is completed.
- If the player completing a shared goal is boosted, that player receives three while the other members receive one.

The Enter [Capacity](../capacity.md) World button shows the total amount earned through this world. Use `/perks` to see how rewards affect your [rank](../ranks.md).
