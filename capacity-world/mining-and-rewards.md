# Mining, Counters, and Rewards

## The Basic Mining Loop

By default, every ten eligible blocks you mine complete one [counter](counters.md) goal and award one [Capacity](../capacity.md). The action bar above your hotbar shows your current [counter](counters.md) progress.

When a goal is completed, the [counter](counters.md) returns to zero and the reward is added immediately. Progress beyond the goal does not carry into the next goal.

## Blocks That Count

Most blocks that can be mined normally in the [Capacity](../capacity.md) World count. A break must succeed; cancelled block breaks do not advance the [counter](counters.md).

Cobblestone and Basalt are specifically excluded. Mining either one gives no [counter](counters.md) progress.

## Capacity Rewards

- A normal completed goal awards one [Capacity](../capacity.md).
- An active [Counter Boost](boosts-and-notifications.md) awards three [Capacity](../capacity.md) to the boosted player instead.
- [Co-op partners](coop.md) normally receive one [Capacity](../capacity.md) when the shared goal is completed.
- [Capacity](../capacity.md) earned here immediately changes your Master Chest limit and [rank](../ranks.md) progress.

The Enter [Capacity](../capacity.md) World button records and displays the total [Capacity](../capacity.md) you have earned through this world.

## Counter Reduction

[Counter Reduction](counter-reduction.md) permanently lowers your personal block goal. It begins at ten eligible blocks and can eventually be reduced to one block per goal.

In a [co-op](coop.md), the shared [counter](counters.md) uses the current group owner's goal. See [Co-op Mining](coop.md) before planning upgrades with a group.

## Lucky Break

[Lucky Break](lucky-break.md) applies to blocks you mine manually. When it triggers, that block contributes a random one to five points of [counter](counters.md) progress instead of the usual one.

A trigger can still roll only one point. Any amount that passes the current goal is discarded when the [counter](counters.md) resets, so a large roll near the end of a goal may not provide its full possible value.

[Lucky Break](lucky-break.md) does not apply to blocks mined by [Chunk Drills](chunk-drills.md).

## Chunk Drill Progress

Every eligible solid block removed by one of your [Chunk Drills](chunk-drills.md) adds exactly one point to the relevant [counter](counters.md). [Counter Reduction](counter-reduction.md), [co-op rewards](coop.md), and an active [Counter Boost](boosts-and-notifications.md) still affect completed goals, but [Lucky Break](lucky-break.md) does not multiply drill progress.

## Checking Progress

- Watch the action bar for the current [counter](counters.md) and active boost time.
- Watch the [Capacity](../capacity.md) World scoreboard for the next reset.
- Use `/perks` to see how earned [Capacity](../capacity.md) affects your [rank](../ranks.md).
- Use `/playtime` or Master Chest Shortcuts to check your current storage limit and available space.
