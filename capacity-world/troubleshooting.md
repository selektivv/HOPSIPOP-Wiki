# Mining Troubleshooting

## `/cw` Is Locked

Reach Atom [rank](../ranks.md). Check the live requirement in `/perks`; spending [Capacity](../capacity.md) can lower your [rank](../ranks.md).

## A Block Did Not Count

Cobblestone, Basalt, air, fluids, Bedrock, barriers, and cancelled breaks do not add [counter](counters.md) progress.

## Co-op Progress Is Separate

Another member must be online inside the mining world. Otherwise, personal [counter](counters.md) progress is used.

## A Drill Cannot Be Placed

Buy the unlock, use the discovered recipe, enter the [Capacity](../capacity.md) World, and choose a chunk without one of your active drills. The limit is four active drills.

## Drill Drops Are Missing

Check free Master Chest [Capacity](../capacity.md). Container contents also require Quasar [rank](../ranks.md) and the global collection setting.

## A Drill Disappeared

Check the Master Chest after completion, menu collection, or a [World Reset](resets.md). A manually broken drill drops at its location.
