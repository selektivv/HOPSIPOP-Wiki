# Troubleshooting and Safety

## `/cw` Does Not Let Me Enter

[Capacity](../capacity.md) World access requires Atom [rank](../ranks.md). Open `/perks` and check [Rank Overview](../ranks.md) for the live requirement. Remember that buying upgrades or claim area spends actual [Capacity](../capacity.md) and can lower your current [rank](../ranks.md).

## A Mined Block Did Not Advance the Counter

- Cobblestone and Basalt never count.
- A cancelled or protected block break does not count.
- Make sure you are inside the [Capacity](../capacity.md) World.
- [Chunk Drill](chunk-drills.md) fluids, air, Bedrock, and barriers do not provide normal block progress.

## My Lucky Break Did Not Add Much

A [Lucky Break](lucky-break.md) trigger adds a random one to five points, so it can roll one. Excess progress is discarded when the [counter](counters.md) goal completes. [Lucky Break](lucky-break.md) also applies only to manual mining, not [Chunk Drills](chunk-drills.md).

## I Cannot Invite Someone to a Co-op

- The other player must be online.
- Neither player can already be in another [co-op](coop.md).
- Both players need enough [Co-op Size](coop.md#requirements) for the resulting group.
- The invitation expires after 60 seconds.

If a reset just occurred or someone left the world, create the [co-op](coop.md) again.

## My Counter Is Not Shared

Shared progress requires at least one other group member to be online and inside the [Capacity](../capacity.md) World. When you are the only active member there, your mining uses your personal [counter](counters.md).

## I Cannot Craft or Place a Chunk Drill

- Buy the one-time [Chunk Drill](chunk-drills.md) unlock in `/cw` first.
- Use the discovered recipe exactly as shown in [Chunk Drills](chunk-drills.md).
- Place the item only in the [Capacity](../capacity.md) World.
- You cannot exceed four active drills.
- You cannot place a second one of your drills in the same chunk.

## Drill Drops Are Missing

Drill output is transferred to your MasterChest, not your inventory. Check the network with `/mc`.

If the network was full or over its current [Capacity](../capacity.md) limit, output may not have fitted. Make free space before operating drills. Container contents also require the Quasar [rank](../ranks.md) global collection switch to be enabled before the container is broken.

## The Fluid Collector Did Not Produce a Bucket

The collector needs an empty bucket in your MasterChest for every water or lava source block. It does not collect flowing fluids. Confirm that the upgrade is enabled in `/chunkdrill` and that the network has room for the filled bucket.

## My Drill Disappeared

Check your MasterChest first. A finished drill, a drill collected through the menu, and an active drill caught by a world reset are returned there. If you broke the drill manually, its item drops at the drill's location instead.

## I Spent Too Much Capacity

[Capacity](../capacity.md) purchases can reduce your storage limit below the number of items already stored. The existing items remain, but deposits may fail. Remove items or earn more [Capacity](../capacity.md) until your stored total is below the limit again.
