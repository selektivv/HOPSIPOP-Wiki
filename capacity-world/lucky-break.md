# Lucky Break

Lucky Break is a permanent mining-world upgrade that gives manually mined eligible blocks a chance to add extra [counter](counters.md) progress.

## How It Works

Without a trigger, an eligible block adds one point. When Lucky Break triggers, that block adds a random one to five points instead.

A successful trigger can still roll only one point. Any progress beyond the current goal is discarded when the goal completes, so a large roll near the end may not provide its full possible value.

## Buying Levels

Open `/cw` and select Lucky Break. The first level costs 1,000 [Capacity](../capacity.md) and gives a 1% trigger chance. Each later level:

- costs 200 [Capacity](../capacity.md) more than the previous level;
- adds 0.2 percentage points to the chance.

The maximum is level 496 with a 100% trigger chance. Purchases are permanent and are not refunded.

## What It Does Not Affect

- It applies only to blocks mined manually.
- It does not multiply blocks processed by [Chunk Drills](chunk-drills.md).
- It does not change which blocks are eligible.
- It changes progress, not the [Capacity](../capacity.md) reward for a completed goal.

Use a [Counter Boost](boosts-and-notifications.md) when you want to increase the reward instead.

## Co-op Behavior

Lucky Break progress contributes to an active [co-op](coop.md) group's shared [counter](counters.md). The trigger chance belongs to the player who mines the block, while the shared goal is determined by the group owner.

## Before Buying

Levels spend the same [Capacity](../capacity.md) used for Master Chest storage and [ranks](../ranks.md). Check your remaining storage room and [rank](../ranks.md) requirements before purchasing many levels at once.
