# Counters

The mining counter tracks progress toward repeatable [Capacity](../capacity.md) rewards in the temporary mining world. By default, ten eligible blocks complete one goal and award one [Capacity](../capacity.md).

## Checking Counter Progress

Your action bar shows the current progress and the number of points required. If a [Counter Boost](boosts-and-notifications.md) is active, its remaining time appears beside the progress.

The counter returns to zero whenever a goal is completed. Extra points beyond the current goal do not carry into the next goal.

## Adding Counter Progress

- A normally mined eligible block adds one point.
- [Lucky Break](lucky-break.md) can make a manually mined block add between one and five points.
- Each eligible block processed by a [Chunk Drill](chunk-drills.md) adds exactly one point.
- Cobblestone, Basalt, air, fluids, Bedrock, barriers, and unsuccessful block breaks do not provide normal progress.

See [Mining and Rewards](mining-and-rewards.md) for eligible-block rules and reward behavior.

## Changing the Goal

[Counter Reduction](counter-reduction.md) permanently lowers your personal goal from the default ten blocks, eventually reaching one block per completed goal.

In a [co-op](coop.md), active members share the group owner's goal and progress. The initial owner is the player with the lower block requirement. When no partner is active inside the world, your personal counter and goal are used instead.

## Reward Modifiers

A completed goal normally awards one [Capacity](../capacity.md). A [Counter Boost](boosts-and-notifications.md) changes the boosted player's reward to three, but it does not change the goal or add counter points.

## What a World Reset Removes

Current counter progress and active [co-op](coop.md) groups are temporary and are cleared during a [world reset](world-rules-and-resets.md). Purchased upgrades, earned [Capacity](../capacity.md), and active boost expiry times remain.

## Continue Learning

- Lower the goal with [Counter Reduction](counter-reduction.md).
- Add occasional extra manual progress with [Lucky Break](lucky-break.md).
- Share progress through [co-op mining](coop.md).
- Automate eligible block progress with [Chunk Drills](chunk-drills.md).
