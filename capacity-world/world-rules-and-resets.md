# World Rules and Resets

## Temporary World

The Capacity World is rebuilt every ten hours. The current countdown appears on the Capacity World scoreboard and continues across server restarts.

Treat every block, container, structure, and location in this world as temporary. The reset removes the world terrain and recreates it from scratch.

## What Happens During a Reset

1. The server announces that the Capacity World is resetting.
2. Active Chunk Drills are stopped and returned to their owners' MasterChests.
3. Players inside the world are teleported to the lobby.
4. Capacity World co-ops are dissolved.
5. Current mining counters are cleared.
6. The old terrain is discarded and a fresh world is created.

Capacity already earned, permanent upgrades, active boost expiry times, personal settings, and items stored outside the temporary terrain are not part of the world wipe.

## Chunk Drills at Reset Time

Drills are automatically collected into the owner's MasterChest before the terrain is removed. This transfer still needs free storage Capacity. Do not leave a full or over-limit MasterChest when the reset is approaching.

## Claims and Building

Claims cannot be created in the Capacity World. The world is for temporary mining and exploration, not permanent bases.

Outside the Capacity World, Capacity is also used as the land-claim resource. Creating or expanding claims spends Capacity, while deleting or shrinking claims refunds the released area. As with Capacity World upgrades, claim spending can reduce MasterChest space and rank progress.

## World Conditions

- PvP is enabled.
- Keep Inventory is enabled.
- The normal daylight cycle is enabled.
- The world border extends 10,000 blocks from the center in every direction.
- Claim creation is blocked.
- Terrain and player-built structures are erased on reset.

## Reset Checklist

- Check the scoreboard countdown regularly.
- Remove anything valuable from placed containers.
- Collect drills early if your MasterChest is close to full.
- Do not use the world for permanent storage or builds.
- Expect to recreate your co-op after the reset.
