# World Resets

The [Capacity](../capacity.md) World is recreated every ten hours. The scoreboard shows the remaining time.

## What the Reset Removes

Terrain, builds, placed containers, current [counters](counters.md), and active [co-op](coop.md) groups are removed. Earned [Capacity](../capacity.md), purchased upgrades, and Master Chest contents remain.

Active [Chunk Drills](chunk-drills.md) are stopped and returned to their owners' Master Chests before the terrain is deleted. The transfer still needs free storage [Capacity](../capacity.md).

## Before the Timer Ends

- Remove valuables from placed containers.
- Leave room for returning drills.
- Collect drills early if storage is nearly full.
- Expect to recreate your [co-op](coop.md) afterward.
