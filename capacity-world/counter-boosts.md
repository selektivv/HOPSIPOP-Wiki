# Counter Boosts

A Counter Boost temporarily changes your personal reward from one to three [Capacity](../capacity.md) when a [counter](counters.md) goal is completed.

## Using a Voucher

Hold the voucher and right-click it. Available durations are 5, 8, 10, 12, 15, 20, 25, and 30 minutes.

The timer continues while you are outside the world or offline. Use a voucher only when you or your [Chunk Drills](chunk-drills.md) are ready to mine.

## Reward Behavior

The boost changes the reward, not the block goal. In a [co-op](coop.md), only the boosted player receives three; other members still receive one.
