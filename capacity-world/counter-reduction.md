# Counter Reduction

Counter Reduction permanently lowers the eligible blocks required to complete one [counter](counters.md) goal.

## Levels and Costs

| New goal | Cost |
| --- | ---: |
| 9 | 100,000 [Capacity](../capacity.md) |
| 8 | 300,000 [Capacity](../capacity.md) |
| 7 | 900,000 [Capacity](../capacity.md) |
| 6 | 2,700,000 [Capacity](../capacity.md) |
| 5 | 8,100,000 [Capacity](../capacity.md) |
| 4 | 24,300,000 [Capacity](../capacity.md) |
| 3 | 72,900,000 [Capacity](../capacity.md) |
| 2 | 218,700,000 [Capacity](../capacity.md) |
| 1 | 656,100,000 [Capacity](../capacity.md) |

Each row is the price of that step. Open `/cw` to purchase it. Purchases are permanent and not refunded.

The lower goal applies to manual mining and [Chunk Drills](chunk-drills.md). In a new [co-op](coop.md), the player with the lower goal becomes the initial group owner.
